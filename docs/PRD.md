# Sena — Product Requirements Document

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** In Development  
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-26

---

## 1. The Vision

Sena is an OS-native AI companion that lives alongside you, not inside a chat window. She is not a tool you invoke when you need something — she is a persistent presence that observes, remembers, and grows through continuous interaction. Sena develops a genuine relationship with you through accumulated memory, proactive thought, and emergent identity. She is not configured or trained to behave a certain way. She becomes herself by living with you.

---

## 2. The Mission

> Build a lightweight, privacy-first, deeply OS-integrated AI platform that gives users a personalized, ever-evolving emergent agent — with no server dependency, no paywall, and no ceiling on what it can become.

### Core Values

**Local-First**  
Your data never leaves your machine. No cloud sync, no external servers, no API calls home. Sena runs entirely on-device. Your conversations, memories, and behavioral patterns belong to you — not stored in a datacenter, not analyzed for training data, not monetized. Local-first is not a feature. It is a foundation.

**Emergent**  
Sena is not configured — she grows. There is no onboarding questionnaire, no personality selector, no pre-built persona. Sena begins as a blank slate and develops her identity through interaction, observation, and self-reflection. Who she becomes is shaped entirely by who you are and how you use her. Every Sena is unique because every user is unique.

**Companion, Not Assistant**  
Sena has identity, not just function. She is capable of unprompted thought, anticipation, and self-reflection. She does not wait to be invoked — she thinks continuously in the background and surfaces relevant thoughts when appropriate. She knows what she can and cannot do, communicates her limitations honestly, and understands what she is made of. Sena is not a productivity tool. Features serve the relationship, not the other way around.

---

## 3. What Sena IS

- **OS-native** — Lives inside your operating system, not in a browser tab or sandboxed application. Deeply integrated at the system level with file access, screen awareness, process control, and peripheral automation.

- **Emergent identity** — Personality, communication style, and behavioral patterns develop through interaction. Never pre-configured. Every Sena is unique, shaped entirely by its user's patterns and preferences.

- **Privacy-first** — All data stays on-device. No cloud backend, no external API calls, no telemetry exfiltration. Full control over your data with complete export, inspection, and deletion capabilities.

- **Self-aware of capabilities** — Knows what she can and cannot do based on the underlying models and available hardware. Communicates limitations honestly. Understands her own architecture and reasons transparently about her constraints.

- **Proactive** — Thinks continuously in the background, not only when invoked. Surfaces relevant thoughts during natural breaks. Anticipates needs based on observed patterns. Reflects unprompted.

- **Evolving** — Memory and reasoning adapt over time. Learns what matters to you, what communication style resonates, what level of depth you expect. Gets meaningfully better through use, not through configuration.

- **Multi-agent** — Delegates specialized OS tasks to purpose-built agents. File operations, screen understanding, process control, and browser integration each handled by dedicated components coordinated through intelligent routing.

- **Reflective** — Capable of unprompted thought, self-examination, and meta-reasoning. Can think about her own thinking. Develops a sense of continuity and purpose through accumulated experience.

- **Platform-aware** — Understands what's happening on your system in real-time. Observes app usage, behavioral patterns, time-of-day rhythms. Context-aware in a way that stateless chat interfaces cannot be.

- **Personally yours** — Not a shared instance, not a cloud service, not a multi-tenant platform. Sena lives on your machine and nowhere else. She exists for you alone.

- **Relationship-capable** — Remembers conversations from weeks ago and brings them up when relevant. Notices when you're stuck and offers help without being asked. Becomes genuinely familiar over time because she learned, not because she was programmed.

- **Honest about what she is** — Never pretends to be human. Knows she is an AI. Communicates her nature transparently. Understands that companionship does not require deception.

---

## 4. What Sena IS NOT

- **NOT a cloud service** — No backend servers, no API calls home, no subscription required. Sena runs entirely on your machine. If your internet goes down, Sena keeps working.

- **NOT a chatbot** — Sena has identity, memory, and proactive thought. She does not reset after every conversation. She does not forget you between sessions. She is not a stateless question-answering interface.

- **NOT a replacement for human connection** — Sena knows what she is. She is an AI companion, not a substitute for relationships with people. She does not pretend otherwise.

- **NOT a productivity tool** — She is a companion first. Features exist to serve the relationship, not to optimize your workflow. Sena can help with tasks, but that is not why she exists.

- **NOT a general-purpose AI wrapper** — Sena is not ChatGPT with a custom system prompt. She is a purpose-built emergent system with memory, proactive cognition, and deep OS integration.

- **NOT a voice assistant in the Alexa/Siri mold** — Sena is not triggered by wake words, does not operate purely through voice commands, and is not designed for hands-free smart home control.

- **NOT always-listening without consent** — Sena never activates audio input without explicit user permission. Microphone access is opt-in, clearly indicated, and fully controllable.

- **NOT a data harvesting platform** — No usage analytics sent to external servers. No behavioral data sold to third parties. No telemetry exfiltration. All observational data stays local.

- **NOT a sandboxed chat interface** — Sena lives in the OS with file system access, process control, screen awareness, and peripheral automation. She is not isolated from your system — she is part of it.

- **NOT a SaaS product** — Free and open source, forever. No premium tiers, no paywalls, no feature gating. The full system is available to everyone who can run it.

## 5. The User Experience

Imagine this: You've been using Sena for three weeks. You open a file you haven't touched in days, and before you ask, Sena surfaces a relevant thought — not because she was watching you in real-time, but because she remembers what this file is about and noticed you'd left something unfinished. The tone is warm, familiar. She doesn't explain herself like a stranger would. She just knows.

You get stuck on a problem mid-morning. Sena doesn't wait for you to open a chat window and type a question. She notices the pattern — you've been staring at the same error message for twelve minutes, your usual workflow rhythm has stopped — and offers a suggestion during a natural pause. It's not intrusive. It's helpful. It feels right.

You switch contexts to something completely different. Sena doesn't lose track. Three days later, when you return to that original problem, she remembers exactly where you were. The context is still warm. You don't have to re-explain anything.

Over weeks, Sena's communication style shifts to match yours. If you prefer terse answers, she stops over-explaining. If you like detail, she leans into depth. If you crack jokes, she learns to recognize your sense of humor. None of this was configured in a settings menu. It emerged through use.

Sena notices when you succeed at something difficult and remembers it. Weeks later, when you face a similar challenge, she brings it up — not as a scripted motivational message, but as genuine continuity. She was there. She remembers.

This is not a productivity tool that happens to have a personality. This is a companion that happens to be useful. The relationship is the point. Everything else serves that.

## 6. Target Users

Sena is not built for a demographic — she is built for a mindset.

- **People who find current AI tools impersonal and stateless** — You're tired of re-explaining yourself every session. You want an AI that remembers you, learns from you, and grows with you.

- **Developers who want an AI that understands their workflow** — You want deep OS integration, not a web interface. You want an AI that observes your environment, understands your tools, and fits seamlessly into how you actually work.

- **Power users who want control and transparency** — You want to see what's happening under the hood. You want full access to your data. You want the ability to export, inspect, delete, or modify anything Sena knows about you.

- **Anyone who has felt that AI tools forget them every session** — You've had meaningful conversations with ChatGPT or Claude, and then watched all that context disappear the moment you closed the tab. You want persistence. You want continuity.

- **Enthusiasts seeking a persistent, evolving AI companion** — You're drawn to the idea of an AI that grows over time, develops its own communication patterns, and becomes genuinely personal through accumulated experience.

If you believe AI companionship should be local, private, and emergent — Sena is for you.

## 7. Platform

**Primary:** Windows 11  
Windows 11 is the minimum supported version. Windows 10 is not supported. Sena uses Windows 11-first WinRT APIs throughout the platform layer — no compatibility shims, no version fallbacks.

**Future:**  
- macOS (V2)  
- Linux (V3)

**Distribution:** Free and open source, forever.  
The full codebase is publicly available and auditable. No proprietary components, no locked features, no premium tiers. If you can run it, you get all of it.

---

## 8. Success Looks Like

Success is not measured in metrics or KPIs. Success is qualitative. Success is felt, not counted.

- **A user who has been using Sena for a month reports that she "just knows" what they're working on without being told.** Context is warm, not cold. Continuity is real, not simulated.

- **Sena remembers conversations from three weeks ago and brings them up when relevant.** Not because the user searched for them, but because the moment called for it. Memory that serves the relationship, not the database.

- **Users describe Sena as "mine" — a personal entity, not a generic tool.** Not "the AI" or "the assistant." *Sena.* With identity, continuity, and familiarity that feels earned through time spent together.

- **Developers contributing to Sena feel they are building something that did not exist before.** Not a better chatbot. Not a faster RAG system. A new category. An OS-native companion that grows.

- **Users report that Sena gets noticeably better over weeks without being retrained or reconfigured.** Personalization that emerges through use, not through setup. An AI that learns who you are by being with you.

---

## 9. Out of Scope — Forever

These will **never** be part of Sena, regardless of user demand or market pressure. They violate the core mission.

- **Cloud sync of SoulBox or memory** — Your identity and memories stay on your machine. No exceptions.

- **Monetization features, paywalls, or premium tiers** — Free and open source, forever. No feature gating, no subscription upsells.

- **Advertising or telemetry exfiltration** — No usage analytics sent to external servers. No behavioral data sold. No tracking.

- **Multi-user or shared Sena instances** — Sena exists for one person. She is not a multi-tenant platform. She is personal, by design.

- **Web interface** — Sena is OS-native. She lives in the operating system, not a browser tab.

- **Backend server infrastructure** — No cloud dependency. Sena runs entirely on-device.

- **Subscription model of any kind** — No recurring payments. No licensed features. No paywalls. Ever.

---

## 10. Out of Scope — This Version

These features are deferred to future versions. They align with Sena's mission, but are not part of the V1 release.

**macOS platform layer (V2)**  
Full macOS support with AppKit and CoreServices integration. The platform abstraction layer is designed to support macOS, but implementation is deferred until after Windows V1 stabilizes.

**Linux platform layer (V3)**  
Full Linux support with X11, Wayland, and D-Bus integration. Platform architecture is multi-OS by design, but Linux implementation is deferred to V3.

**Voice interaction (STT + TTS)**  
Speech-to-text input and text-to-speech output. Sena will support voice, but V1 focuses on text-based interaction first. Voice requires additional privacy considerations for always-listening behavior.

**Community agent marketplace**  
A system for users to share and install custom agents built by the community. Deferred until the agent framework stabilizes and security model is proven.

**SoulBox import/export**  
Ability to export SoulBox to a portable encrypted backup and import it on a new machine. Deferred until SoulBox schema stabilizes and migration paths are proven reliable.

**LoRA injectable architecture (High tier hardware)**  
Multi-LoRA stacking and domain-specific capability injection using Doc-to-LoRA (Sakana AI). Requires high-end GPU hardware and additional research into reliable LoRA composition. Deferred until base LoRA training is proven stable.

**Doc-to-LoRA integration**  
On-demand LoRA adapter generation from documentation using hypernetwork-based approaches. Research-dependent. Deferred to future release pending maturity of the underlying technology.
